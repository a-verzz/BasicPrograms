Some useful options for grep include:

-i: Perform a case-insensitive search.
-v: Invert the match, displaying lines that do not contain the pattern.
-r or -R: Recursively search files in directories.
-n: Show line numbers for matching lines.
-w: Match whole words only.
-A num: Print num lines of trailing context after a match.
-B num: Print num lines of leading context before a match.
-C num: Print num lines of leading and trailing context around a match.
